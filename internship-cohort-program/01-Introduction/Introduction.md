## Heading tag in HTML

 In HTML heading are title or subtitle that are used to display on a webpage.There are six heading elements. These elements are h1,h2,h3,h4,h5,h6. h1 is the High and h6 is low.

```html
    <h1> Heading 1 </h1>
    <h2> Heading 2 </h2>
    <h3> Heading 3 </h3>
    <h4> Heading 4 </h4>
    <h5> Heading 5 </h5>
    <h6> Heading 6 </h6>
```
